Who We Are

Abihani Express is the premier online destination for premium leather goods in Yobe State, Nigeria. We specialize in handcrafted leather shoes, bags, and accessories that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern style.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, durable, and stylish leather products while supporting local artisans and growing the community economy.

Meet Abihani Isa

A young entrepreneur with big dreams, Abihani Isa started this journey to bring quality leather products to his community. He's also a published author, with a novel chronicling his journey of love with Ummihani.
